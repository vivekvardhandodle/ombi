We are Hiring


ARE YOU EXPERIENCED IN DRIVING, CUSTOMER SERVICE OR PATIENT CARE? JOIN THE SUCCESS TEAM AT VA TRANSPORTATION HEALTHCARE LLC

This role centers around non-emergency medical transportation, and we encourage applicants with backgrounds in customer service, home health-care, and delivering exceptional patient or similar care. VA Transportation Healthcare is dedicated to serving our communities through ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) transportation. Join our team, the leading private-sector provider of public transportation in North America, demonstrating genuine concern for our employees, passengers, and the communities we serve.

WHY DRIVE WITH US?

  • Top paid
  • Good Sign-On Bonus for 6 months empl
  • Paid Training on the job
  • Starting salary $20 hour and up to $25 per hour with prior experience and passes the required process.
  • Fixed schedule/routine routes
  • Ability to advance your career

Apply today for immediate consideration!


RESPONSIBILITIES:

Proficiency in safely and courteously transporting passengers across the regions of Maryland, Virginia, and Washington D.C.
Welcoming passengers with greetings, overseeing fare payments, and upholding a secure atmosphere. Shifts will be scheduled according to operational requirements, either at 6 AM or 5 PM on weekdays, with occasional weekends. All employees must be available for the designated shifts, encompassing weekdays, evenings, and weekends.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Applicants must be aged 21 or above and possess a high school diploma, GED, or an equivalent qualification.
  • A clean driving history in either MD, DC, or VA is required.
  • You should be capable of furnishing a certified driving record spanning five years. This document can be acquired through your local Motor Vehicle Authority or obtained on-site at our facility for a nominal fee.
  • Demonstration of outstanding customer service and communication abilities is essential.
  • Punctuality is of paramount importance, with a strong emphasis on consistent attendance.
  • Mandatory compliance includes undergoing drug testing and an in-depth criminal background check.
  • Capability to work in varying weather conditions is required.
  • Successful completion of driver training and obtaining certification is necessary.
  • Physical Requirements Include Ability to:
    • Be capable of remaining seated for extended durations.
    • Lift, push, pull, and carry tools, objects, or equipment above shoulder level unaided.
    • Move up and down stairs easily
    • Perform tasks involving overhead reaching and kneeling, including bending, twisting, pulling, and stooping.
