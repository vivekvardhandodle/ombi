About Bright Star Non Medical Transportation

A Small Introduction

Welcome to the Bright Star Non Medical Transportation



Call Us Now Download Business profile & Prices

Started in 2022 as a family owned business, we provide professional, door-to-door non-emergency wheelchair transport which is available to the public throughout the tri-state area. Our specialized medical transportation responsibilities include but are not limited to: senior citizens, individuals who require the use of wheelchairs and parents and children with special needs. Our nemt services are for private citizens, hospitals, doctors, insurance companies/HMOs, nursing or private group homes, and assisted living facilities. We assist clients from the time of pick-up to drop-off and make sure that they get there safely and on-time.
